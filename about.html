About us

We build the AI that runs the last mile.

Tangering started inside a logistics operation, not a research lab. Every line of our product is shaped by the same operators who used to make those calls themselves.

For most of Latin America, the last mile is still answered by a person with a headset. We watched ops teams burn out, customers go uncontacted, and shipments fail not because the truck broke down, but because nobody picked up the phone.

So we built an AI Operating System that talks to every customer, in their language, the moment it matters. Today, Tangering handles confirmations, exceptions, support and operations across logistics, real estate, utilities and insurance, keeping millions of conversations alive 24/7.

We're a small team obsessed with one number: deliveries that land on the first attempt. Everything else, the voice, the flows, the integrations, exists to move that number up.
