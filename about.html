Our story

Started in the warehouse, not the lab.

We work with large companies where the last mile is still answered by a person with a headset. Ops teams burning out, customers going uncontacted, shipments failing because nobody picked up the phone.

01

The problem we kept seeing.

At enterprise scale, last mile operations still depend on humans dialing one customer at a time. That's why deliveries fail, not because the truck broke down, but because nobody picked up.

02

What we built.

An AI Operating System that talks to every customer, in their language, the moment it matters. Confirmations, exceptions, support and operations across logistics, real estate, utilities and insurance, keeping millions of conversations alive 24/7.

03

What we measure.

One number obsesses us: deliveries that land on the first attempt. The voice, the flows, the integrations all exist to move that number up.
