Pricing, answered.

What counts as a call?

Any conversation initiated or received by a Tangering agent, voice or messaging, regardless of length. Failed connects are not billed.

Can I bring my own carrier or twilio number?

Yes. Tangering works with your existing telephony provider; we charge only for the platform, not for the minutes.

Is there a minimum commitment?

Starter is month-to-month with no minimum. Growth runs on annual or monthly billing. Enterprise contracts are custom.

How quickly can we go live?

Most Growth customers ship their first production agent in under three weeks. Our FDEs handle integrations end to end.

What languages are supported?

Spanish (LATAM & Iberian), Portuguese (BR & PT), English. Other languages on request.
