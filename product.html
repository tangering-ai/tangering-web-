01

Agent builder & versioning

Copilot-built agent
Describe the process in natural language; it generates the agent and the prompt, then drops you into a canvas to fine-tune.
Prompt guidelines
The prompt copilot follows each LLM provider's own prompting guidelines, and proposes changes you accept or reject one by one.
Git-style versioning
Save drafts without publishing, major / minor / patch versions, test any version before publish, one-click rollback.
02

Models, transcription & voice

Model routing
Via OpenRouter for reliability and SLA. Available models: Anthropic Haiku, GPT-5.4 mini/nano, DeepSeek, Nemotron. Unlimited agents; new client models can be added through an evaluation stage for latency.
Transcription
Tangering's own model or Deepgram (recommended for production). Per-agent key terms for hard-to-transcribe names: up to 100 terms, max 2 words each.
Voice
ElevenLabs with tunable stability, similarity and speed. Direct in-app voice selection is in progress; for now voices are added on request.

03

Data security

Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Sensitive fields are masked by default, access is role-controlled, and every action is audit-logged. We use your data only to run your operation, never sold, never reused to train anyone else's models.

Recordings retained ~2 years and accessible in-platform. Shared report links are signed and expiring, max one week.

Voice quality in the real world

Voice isolation
For bad-audio environments: drivers on speakerphone, music playing, poor signal.
Answering-machine detection
Adjustable threshold; configurable interruption sensitivity (how many words interrupt the agent); silence handling (nudge, then hang up).

A built-in voice stack (real-time telephony, native audio processing) is why this actually works in production, not a wrapper on top of a generic model.

Tools, integrations & telephony

Custom tools & MCP

06

Monitoring, reporting & access

Live monitoring
Active calls and messages in real time, one-click human takeover, alerts when a conversation needs attention.
Reports
Scheduled, webhook (fires on call hangup, pushes call analysis to the client's endpoint) and period analysis. A metrics copilot generates dashboards and KPIs, and can email daily summaries.
