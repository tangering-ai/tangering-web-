Agents

Your agent ready in minutes, not weeks.

Describe the process you want to automate. Tangering turns it into a configured agent, with voice, flow and rules, no code required.

  • Build voice and WhatsApp agents with a guided chat
  • Connect external tools: CRM, webhooks, APIs
  • Edit any part of the agent with the AI Copilot

You don't need to be technical to build an agent that sounds professional and runs complex processes.

Campaigns

Call a thousand customers like they were one.

Upload your list, set the schedule and let the agent do the work. Every call confirmed, every response logged, automatically.

  • Outbound voice campaigns or hybrid (voice + WhatsApp)
  • Contact prioritization and automatic retries
  • Real-time results as calls happen

An agent running 10,000 calls a day doesn't make mistakes, doesn't get tired and doesn't call outside business hours.

Telephony

Use your infrastructure or ours.

If you already have a SIP trunk, plug it in in minutes. If not, use Tangering's numbers and start calling today.

  • Connect your existing SIP trunk without friction
  • Buy local numbers directly from the platform
  • Inbound and outbound calls on the same number

No separate telephony contracts, no complex integrations, no calls to your IT team.

Monitoring

See every conversation while it happens.

Listen to live calls, read messages in real time and take over when a customer needs a human, without the customer noticing.

  • Real-time panel of active calls and messages
  • Take over any conversation with one click
  • Automatic alerts when a conversation needs attention

Your team only steps in when it matters. The agent handles the rest.

Transfers

The agent knows when to pass to a human.

Set the conditions. When the customer needs it, the agent transfers the call to the right team, with the full conversation context.

  • Conditional transfers based on customer intent
  • Transfer to SIP extensions, phone numbers or internal agents
  • The agent briefs the customer before transferring, no abrupt cuts

Without the customer having to repeat what they already explained.

Reports

Every call documented. Ready for your team.

Generate reports with the exact information you need: transcript, outcome, captured data, duration. Export and share with whoever needs it.

  • Reports configurable by agent, campaign and date range
  • Pick exactly which columns to include in each report
  • AI-extracted data: intent, promised amount, rating

Your team has the information to improve every week, not every quarter.
