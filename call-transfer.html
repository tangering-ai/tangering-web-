01 — Warm transfer

Brief before you bridge.

Before the call routes, the human gets a summarized brief: who's calling, what they need, what's been promised, and what's already in the CRM.

02 — Smart routing

The right human, not just any.

Route by language, skill, region or shift. Tangering pulls from your existing queue, ACD or Slack groups so you keep the rules you already have.

03 — Live handoff

Zero awkward repeats.

The customer doesn't have to re-explain. The human picks up with the AI on the line for a beat, then takes over cleanly.

04 — Full audit trail

Every transfer, every reason.

Track which intents trigger transfers, which agents resolve them, and how long the handoff actually takes — per route, per shift, per customer.

05 — Cost guardrails

Only transfer when it pays off.

Set thresholds so the AI handles low-risk paths end-to-end and only escalates when the lifetime value justifies the human minutes.

Salesforce, HubSpot, Zendesk, custom TMS, the post-call summary writes back automatically to the system of record.
