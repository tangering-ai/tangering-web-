How it works

Three steps. No dropped calls.

From AI conversation to human handoff in under two seconds, with the customer never repeating themselves.

Step 01

Agent detects intent

Tangering listens, classifies and decides whether the conversation can be resolved end-to-end or needs human escalation, in milliseconds.

Step 02

Context packet builds

Warm transfer

Brief before you bridge.

Before the call routes, the human gets a structured brief: who's calling, what they need, what's been promised and what's already on the CRM.

  • Auto-generated call summary in under 1 second
  • Full conversation transcript with timestamps
  • CRM fields, order history and previous tickets
  • Reason-for-transfer flagged by the AI
Warm transfer brief panel

Smart routing

The right human, not just any human.

Route by language, skill, region or shift. Tangering plugs into your existing queue, ACD or Slack groups so you keep the rules you already trust.

  • Skill-based routing with custom rules
  • SIP extensions, mobile numbers or internal agents
  • Time-of-day and follow-the-sun strategies
  • Overflow into partner BPOs when your team is saturated
Routing rules editor

Build the rules once. Tangering applies them across every channel, voice, WhatsApp, SMS.

Live handoff

Zero awkward repeats.

The customer doesn't have to re-explain. The human picks up on the live call with the AI still on the line for a beat, then takes over cleanly.

  • Three-way bridge for the handoff moment
  • AI introduces the human and steps out gracefully
  • Optional whisper-only mode for agent coaching
  • Sub-second latency on bridge connect
Live bridge timeline
